Welcome to our Website

Our main objective to create more efficient engineers. Our hands on training and Expert Trainers provide more realistic examples and share their experiences which makes the participant's life easier in the real world.

TSD delivered Accredited Gas, Electrical, Plumbing and Construction training courses in London, enabling students from all over the UK .

We are open 5 days every week and we welcome visitors to our Centre at any time. We do not require you to make an appointment; please feel free to turn up to look at what we do, meet our staff and chat with existing students. From a location point of view we are well situated for road, rail and bus links.

Course categories

Trade Skills Development Ltd. is an engineering skills training company which has been providing top quality, industrially relevant, short, practical electrical, Gas and plumbing courses in the UK. We provide, a comprehensive range of highly-focused technical courses from bench fitting skills to programmable logic controllers A strong emphasis on useful practical skills A hands-on approach to training so that delegates ‘learn by doing’ on modern industrial equipment Competence based.

Gas Engineering

Our gas engineering courses have been Accredited by awarding bodies with our students in mind. Whether you're a new entrant to the industry or you just need to update your existing gas qualifications, TSD can help you.

Electrician

Offering both an NVQ Level 3 Electrical Training course and the Domestic Electrical Installer Award, we have an electrician course to suit you. Designed to meet your needs, train with TSD to gain industry-recognised mentor.

Plumber

Our NVQ Level 2 plumbing course is designed to provide you with the plumbing skills you need in today's market. With both theoretical and practical training, TSD deliver training course that will provide you with the ability to go straight into work.
